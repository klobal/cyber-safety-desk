STAGE 03: Direct Desk Containment & Critical Incident Isolation

🛑 Desk Guide: Active Countermeasures

Do not just take notes—**act immediately** to secure the victim's digital footprint. Walk through these technical mitigation protocols step-by-step. Check off each box as you execute the countermeasure to compile a forensic record of your response.

1. Terminate Attacker Access Matrix

Instruct the citizen to immediately block the hostile numbers across all channels (GSM, WhatsApp, Telegram). Sever the threat actor's direct psychological line of communication.

2. Infrastructure SIM Lock down & IMEI Freeze

If a SIM foot-print hijack is detected, pick up the desk line and contact the mobile operator support group to freeze routing maps, invalidate current ICCIDs, and block downstream financial transfers.

3. Cloud Session Revocation & MFA Reset

Force log out all active web applications linked to the phone line. Revoke Google OAuth tokens, change primary passwords, and set up secondary app-based authenticators rather than relying on SMS pins.

4. Package Forensic Artifacts (Section 106B Compliance)

Export unedited M-Pesa message confirmations, capture headers from phishing emails, log specific destination phone numbers, and take timestamped screenshots. This ensures your evidence stands up under Kenyan law.
